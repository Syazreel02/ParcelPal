FAQs

Here are some of frequently-asked questions regarding our parcel management system!

1. Where can I collect my parcel on campus?

Please collect your parcel at our designated area at Pusat Pelajar when your parcel status is ready!

2. What do I need to bring when collecting my parcel?

When collecting your parcel, make sure to bring a valid student ID and also the slip that shows the Slip ID and Parcel ID. This will help us ensure that the parcel is released to the correct recipient.

3. How will I know when my parcel has arrived on campus?

You can check at our track ID page to see your parcel whether it is ready or not!

4. Can someone else collect my parcel on my behalf?

Unfortunately, we do not allow other students to collect your parcel on behalf due to security measures.
